## Overview

The Participant entity is the core aggregate root of the Participant Manager domain. It represents a person who is eligible for or enrolled in one or more screening pathways.

## Properties

### Identity

- NHS Number (unique identifier)

### Demographics

- Name
- Date of Birth
- Address
- Contact Details

### Status

- Active/Deceased/Opted Out
- Contact Preferences

## Invariants

1. Must have a valid NHS Number
2. Cannot be enrolled in the same pathway multiple times simultaneously
3. Cannot be enrolled if deceased
4. Must have valid demographic information

## Relationships

### One-to-Many

- PathwayEnrollments
- Episodes

## Related Events

### Consumes

- CreateEnrolment
- DeleteEnrolment
- CloseEpisode

### Produces

- EnrolmentCreated
- EnrolmentDeleted
- EpisodeOpened

## Aggregate Boundary

The Participant aggregate includes:

- Participant (root)
- PathwayEnrollments
- Episodes

## Business Rules

1. A participant can only have one active enrollment per pathway
2. A participant's status must be valid for enrollment
3. All demographic information must be complete and valid
4. Contact preferences must be respected for communications
