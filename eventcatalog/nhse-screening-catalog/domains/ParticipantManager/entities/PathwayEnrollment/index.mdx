## Overview

The PathwayEnrollment entity represents the relationship between a Participant and a Pathway. It tracks the participant's enrollment status, history, and opt-out information for a specific screening pathway.

## Properties

### Identity

- Composite key:
  - Participant ID (NHS Number)
  - Pathway ID

### Status Information

- Enrollment status (active, suspended, withdrawn)
- Enrollment date
- Last screening date
- Opt-out status and date

## Invariants

1. Cannot have multiple active enrollments for same participant and pathway
2. Must be associated with a valid participant and pathway
3. Cannot be enrolled if participant is deceased
4. Enrollment dates must be valid and sequential

## Relationships

### Many-to-One

- Participant
- Pathway

### One-to-Many

- Episodes

## Related Events

### Consumes

- CreateEnrolment
- DeleteEnrolment

### Produces

- EnrolmentCreated
- EnrolmentDeleted

## Business Rules

1. A participant can only have one active enrollment per pathway
2. Enrollment status transitions must follow the defined workflow
3. Opt-out status must be tracked and respected
4. Last screening date must be updated when new episodes are created
