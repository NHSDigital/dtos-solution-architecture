## Overview

The Platform domain contains core infrastructure services that are essential for the operation of the entire screening service platform. It includes services for business auditing, service layer management, and pathway coordination.

## Domain Responsibilities

1. Platform Infrastructure

   - Provide core platform services
   - Manage service interactions
   - Ensure platform stability and reliability

2. Service Coordination

   - Coordinate between different service domains
   - Manage service dependencies
   - Handle cross-domain workflows

3. Platform Governance

   - Maintain audit trails
   - Enforce platform policies
   - Monitor platform health

## Domain Services

### Business Audit Services

- **AuditStoreService**: Maintains an immutable audit trail of all events across the screening service domains

### Service Layer Services

- **ServiceLayer**: Manages service interactions, routing, and monitoring

### Pathway Coordinator Services

- **PathwayCoordinator**: Coordinates pathway execution and management across domains

## Domain Events

### Consumes Events

The Platform domain consumes events from all other domains in the system, including:

- Participant Manager Domain events
- Appointment Domain events
- Screening Pathway Domain events
- Results Management Domain events
- Quality Assurance Domain events
- Cohort Manager Domain events
- Campaign Manager Domain events
- Communications Manager Domain events
- Participant Support Domain events
- BI & Data Analysis Domain events
