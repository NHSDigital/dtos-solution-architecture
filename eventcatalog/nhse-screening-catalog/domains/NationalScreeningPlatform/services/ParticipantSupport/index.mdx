## Overview

The Participant Support service is responsible for providing support services to participants throughout their screening journey. It handles queries, concerns, and assistance requests, ensuring participants receive appropriate help and guidance.

## Responsibilities

1. **Support Management**

   - Handle support requests
   - Process participant queries
   - Manage assistance requests
   - Track support cases

2. **Query Resolution**

   - Answer screening questions
   - Provide guidance
   - Handle concerns
   - Manage escalations

## Business Rules

1. All support requests must be logged
2. Queries must be responded to within 24 hours
3. Support cases must be tracked
4. Sensitive information must be protected
5. Support history must be maintained
