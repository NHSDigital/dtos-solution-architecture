## Overview

The Appointment Allocator service is responsible for managing the allocation of screening appointments across the system. It ensures efficient use of available slots while considering participant preferences and service capacity.

## Responsibilities

1. **Appointment Allocation**

   - Match participants with available slots

2. **Slot Optimisation**

   - Maximize slot utilisation
   - Balance demand across time periods
   - Handle priority allocations

## Business Rules

1. Slot utilization should not exceed 95%
2. Failed allocations must trigger automatic retry logic
