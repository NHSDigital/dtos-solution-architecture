## Overview

The Appointment Booker service manages the booking and confirmation process for screening appointments. It ensures that appointments are properly booked, confirmed.

## Responsibilities

1. **Booking Management**

   - Process booking requests
   - Confirm appointments
   - Handle booking modifications
   - Manage cancellations

2. **Integration**
   - Connect with external booking systems
   - Manage booking data
   - Handle booking conflicts
   - Maintain booking records

## Business Rules

1. Bookings must be confirmed within 24 hours
2. All bookings must include participant contact details
3. Reminders must be sent 48 hours before appointment
4. Cancellations must be processed within 4 hours
5. Booking records must be maintained for 7 years
