## Overview

The AppointmentAllocated event is published when the AppointmentAllocator service successfully allocates an appointment slot for a participant. This event indicates that a suitable slot has been found and reserved based on the participant's requirements and available capacity.

## Event Properties

### Required Properties

- `participantId`: Unique identifier of the participant
- `appointmentId`: Unique identifier of the allocated appointment
- `pathwayId`: Identifier of the screening pathway
- `locationId`: Identifier of the screening location
- `appointmentDateTime`: Date and time of the allocated appointment
- `allocationTimestamp`: When the allocation occurred
- `priority`: Priority level of the allocated appointment

### Optional Properties

- `specialRequirements`: Any special requirements for the appointment
- `notes`: Additional notes about the allocation

## Business Rules

1. The appointment slot must be available and not already allocated
2. The allocation must be within pathway-specific timeframes
3. The location must be valid and operational
4. The participant must be eligible for the appointment

## Service Publishing

This event is published by the `AppointmentAllocator` service within the Appointment domain.

## Related Commands

- `AllocateAppointment`: The command that triggered this event
- `BookAppointment`: The next command that may be issued

## Related Events

- `AllocationFailed`: The event that may be published if allocation fails
