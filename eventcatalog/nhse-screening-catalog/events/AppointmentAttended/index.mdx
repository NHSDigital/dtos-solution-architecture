## Overview

The AppointmentAttended event is published when a participant attends their scheduled appointment. This event indicates successful completion of the appointment and may trigger follow-up actions in the screening pathway.

## Event Properties

### Required Properties

- `participantId`: Unique identifier of the participant
- `appointmentId`: Unique identifier of the attended appointment
- `attendanceTimestamp`: When the attendance was recorded
- `attendanceType`: Type of attendance (e.g., in-person, virtual)
- `attendanceStatus`: Status of the attendance (e.g., complete, partial)
- `recordedBy`: Identifier of the person/service recording the attendance

### Optional Properties

- `attendanceNotes`: Additional information about the attendance
- `followUpRequired`: Whether follow-up is needed
- `followUpDetails`: Details of any required follow-up
- `qualityMetrics`: Any quality metrics recorded during attendance

## Business Rules

1. The appointment must exist and be in a booked state
2. The attendance must be recorded within valid timeframes
3. The attendance must be recorded by an authorised person/service
4. All required attendance information must be complete

## Service Publishing

This event is published by the `AppointmentBooker` service within the Appointment domain.

## Related Commands

- `BookAppointment`: The command that led to this event
- `RecordAttendance`: The command that triggered this event
